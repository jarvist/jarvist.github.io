Bulk Hetero Jelly (BHJ)

For my 28th birthday I report on the Bulk Hetero Jelly (BHJ). Unlike conventional Jellies, the BHJ architecture allows for a fully solution processed deposition at lower temperatures and without the vacuum processing necessary for conventional designs. The Molar scale phase separation is achieved implicitly by the kinetically limited dynamics of the slithering snakes and foam strawberries, which are then ‘frozen’ in place by the increasing viscosity of the support matrix.
